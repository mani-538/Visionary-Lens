# Visionary Lens - Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Cover Slide
- **Title:** Visionary Lens
- **Subtitle:** An AI-Powered Zero-Sight Academic Assistant for Visually Impaired Students
- **Details:** Your Name, Roll Number, College Name, Department.

## Slide 2: Problem Statement
- Visually impaired students struggle with printed materials.
- Traditional tools just extract text, not meaning.
- Challenges with diagrams, math formulas, and complex layouts.

## Slide 3: Proposed Solution
- A fully voice-driven mobile ecosystem.
- Combines OCR, LLMs (Gemini), and TTS.
- Features like "Ask My Notes" and "Diagram Explainer".

## Slide 4: Key Features
- **Voice Navigation:** Complete hands-free experience.
- **Smart Scanning:** AI-guided camera alignment.
- **Advanced AI Analysis:** Summaries, Diagram Descriptions, Formula Reading.
- **Study Mode:** Flashcards and Quizzes generated on the fly.

## Slide 5: System Architecture
- **Frontend:** Flutter (Mobile App).
- **Backend:** FastAPI (Python).
- **AI:** Google Gemini 1.5 Flash.
- **Database:** SQLite with SQLAlchemy.

## Slide 6: Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** Provider for state management, Flutter TTS, Speech-To-Text.
- **Backend:** JWT Auth, gTTS, RESTful API.
- **AI Integration:** Google Generative AI Python SDK.

## Slide 7: Working Methodology
1. Image Capture (Mobile).
2. OCR Extraction (FastAPI + Gemini).
3. Contextual Analysis (Summarization/Diagram Description).
4. Audio Output (Frontend/Backend TTS).

## Slide 8: Future Enhancements
- Real-time video processing.
- Support for multiple languages.
- Integration with external LMS (Moodle, Google Classroom).

## Slide 9: Conclusion
- Visionary Lens empowers visually impaired students to study independently.
- Demonstrates the practical power of Generative AI in accessibility.
